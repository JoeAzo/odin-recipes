Karaage

Description

Karaage put simply is Japanese-style fried chicken. It's crispy, juicy, and packed with umami and flavor. Topped with a drizzle of lemon juice and a green onion sauce, it goes well with a nice bowl of steamed white rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigh or Chicken Breast
  • Cornstarch or Potato Starch
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Cooking Sake
  • Rice Wine Vinegar
  • Egg
  • All Purpose Flour

Steps

  1. Chop the chicken thighs into bite sized pieces and put into a bowl.
  2. Pour 1 tbsp of soy sauce, mirin, and cooking sauce into the bowl of chicken.
  3. Mix well and let marinate for 15 minutes.
  4. Once done, beat egg and pour into bowl of chicken
  5. Create cornstarch/flour mixture to batter pieces of chicken
  6. Dredge each piece and let rest on a tray for 5 minutes
  7. Deep fry them and done!
