Scrambled Eggs

Description

These fluffy scrambled eggs are to die for and makes for a great part of your morning!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Scramble 2 eggs into a bowl with a splash of milk.
  2. Warm pan on medium heat for a few minutes and put in tablespoon of oil
  3. Once pan is warm enough, pour egg mixture into pan and continuously stir.
  4. Serve once cooked to desired consistency.
