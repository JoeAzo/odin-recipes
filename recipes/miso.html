Miso Soup

Description

Miso Soup is an incredibly light, yet tasty way to start your morning, and is very quick to make as well. Not a lot of ingredients are required for this and you'll be making it whenever it's cold outside!.

Ingredients

  • Miso Paste
  • Dashi Stock
  • Water

Steps

  1. Get a pot of water and put it on the oven over medium high heat.
  2. Once it gets to a rolling boil, pour 5g of dashi stock into water and mix
  3. Mix in the miso paste to taste.
  4. Plate and serve.
